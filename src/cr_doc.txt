Syntaxe :
	colorize -rgbycmw expr ...
		-r	: colorize in red
		-g	: colorize in green
		-b	: colorize in blue
		-y	: colorize in yellow
		-c	: colorize in cyan
		-m	: colorize in magenta
		-w	: colorize in high white

		expr	:
			text
			^.*text.*$
			text\P		: colorize until the end of the paragraph
						 (until next empty line of EOF)
